Google Gemini Student Offer

Get free Gemini Pro for 15 months + 2TB Google One storage using temporary .edu email

15 min
Duration
Easy
Difficulty
Active
Status

This step-by-step guide will help you claim the Google Gemini Student Offer, which includes free access to Gemini Pro for 15 months and 2TB of Google One storage, even if you don't have a legitimate .edu email.

Prerequisites

  • • A reliable VPN (preferably with US servers)
  • • A temporary .edu email (we'll generate one)
  • • A new Google account

1 Set Up a VPN

Since the Google Gemini student offer may have regional restrictions, you'll need a VPN to bypass them.

  1. 1. Download a VPN (e.g., SetupVPN or any free VPN with US servers).
  2. 2. Connect to a US server (required to access the offer).
  3. 3. Verify your IP location using WhatIsMyIP to ensure it shows a US address.

Note: Some VPNs offer limited free usage—SetupVPN provides 30 minutes free.

2 Create a New Google Account

To avoid conflicts with existing accounts, create a fresh Google account while the VPN is active.

  1. 1. Go to Google Account Creation.
  2. 2. Fill in the details (use a real phone number for verification).
  3. 3. Do not use your primary Google account—this ensures eligibility checks work properly.

3 Generate a Temporary .edu Email

Since the offer requires a student email, we'll use a disposable .edu service.

Option 1: TempMail.io

  1. 1. Visit TempMail.io.
  2. 2. Copy the automatically generated email (or refresh for a new one).
  3. 3. Some versions allow filtering for .edu domains—check if available.

Option 2: TempMail (Chrome Extension)

  1. 1. Install Temp Mail - Edu Email.
  2. 2. Generate a temporary .edu email directly in your browser.

Note: If one service doesn't work, try another—some may be blocked by Google.

4 Claim the Google Gemini Student Offer

Now, apply for the offer using your new Google account and temporary email.

  1. 1. Visit the Google Gemini Student Offer Page (ensure VPN is active).
  2. 2. Click "Get Offer" or similar.
  3. 3. When prompted, enter the temporary .edu email you generated.
  4. 4. Complete verification (if required).

Note: If you see errors, try a different VPN server or a new Google account.

5 Verify & Enjoy the Benefits

  • • Check your Google account for Gemini Pro access.
  • • Confirm 2TB Google One storage in your account settings.
  • • The offer should last 15 months (expires June 30, 2025).

Troubleshooting

  • ❌ "Offer not available in your region" → Switch VPN servers (US only).
  • ❌ "Invalid student email" → Try a different temporary email service.
  • ❌ "Payment method issues" → Use a different Google account.

Final Notes

  • • This method bypasses the need for a real .edu email.
  • • Act fast—the offer may end soon.
  • • Avoid using your primary Google account to prevent restrictions.

Enjoy your free Gemini Pro and Google One storage! 🚀
